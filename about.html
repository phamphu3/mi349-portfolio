About Me

I'm an Information Science student at Michigan State University, interested in how small, intentional design decisions can make the web feel more understandable and human.

Overview

In MI349, I've learned to move from rough ideas and Figma wireframes to fully coded, responsive pages. My work focuses on clean structure, thoughtful copy, and layouts that still hold up on a small phone screen at 2a.m.

Skills & Tools

  • Semantic HTML5 and accessible structure
  • CSS, Flexbox, responsive grid layouts
  • Design systems and prototypes in Figma
  • Copywriting for portfolio and product pages
  • GitHub & Codespaces workflow, Netlify hosting

Design Principles I Use

MI349 emphasized clarity, hierarchy, and responsiveness. I try to:

  • Use type, color, and spacing to guide attention.
  • Keep content scannable with clear headings and short paragraphs.
  • Respect accessibility basics like contrast and proper alt text.
  • Design mobile-first, then enhance on larger screens.
